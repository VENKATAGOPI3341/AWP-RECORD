Cricket is truly the most popular sport in India, and is an outdoor game that is enjoyed by everyone. The game is played by two teams of 11 players, each with a bat and a ball. Cricket is usually played in a big playground. But people love it so much that small teams and groups play it on the roads and wherever there is enough space! Cricket is also my favorite sport to watch. I always sit and watch cricket with my family. In the evenings I go out to play cricket with my friends. We can play cricket all day without getting bored.
